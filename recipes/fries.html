Large Fries!

Who doesn't like fries but strangely who dislikes fried chicken?

I like fries, and Gappy does too but one thing we disagree is on fried chicken, I love fried chicken, now unfortunately our friend gappy does not. Well I guess we can agree to disagree and in this page I'll honor his wish by making it about a recipe for large fries!

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes!
  • Salt
  • Oil

Recipe:

  1. Fries are really simple to make honestly. To begin with you will need a knife to chop the fries, since Gappy loves big fries I will be cutting them into big pieces, but you can feel free to cut them however you like.

  2. After this you need to get a bowl shaped pan and we will deep fry it in that pan. So first of all you'll need to pour oil, pour a lot of oil so that all your french fries would basically be submerged in the oil. After that you need to heat the oil for about 5 minutes and then finally you can add your cut potatoes. So the time you need to wait for them to cook depends on the size of the fries and how crispy you prefer them but ideally you should wait until they are golden brown

  3. After this you take the fries and let them rest on a plate covered by a paper towel which will be used to soak the oil. After the oil is soaked you should add salt for flavor according to your preference, no fries are complete without salt of course ;)

  4. Voila! The large fries recipe is complete, now you can add whatever extra condiments you want, and of course make sure to enjoy your fries!
